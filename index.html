Cars

Cars are an art of creation, designed to transport you from one place to another.

Cars are categorized by where they are from. There are American/Muscle cars, Japanese/JDM, and European. Then they are classified as to what make and model the vehicle is. Most cars run on gasoline, though some run on electricity and diesel. Exotic Vehicles are also vehicles with a particular “twist” added to them; you can find more information on its separate webpage.

European Vehicles

Most Euro cars originate from Europe, though there are some exceptions. Euro Vehicles tend to be more performance-based and luxurious. Maintenance-wise, euro cars are costly and require “long-term” expenses.

JDM

JDM refers to the Japanese Domestic Market. These vehicles are incredibly reliable, and they are highly customizable. JDM vehicles have been used in drift races, as they are easy to work with.
