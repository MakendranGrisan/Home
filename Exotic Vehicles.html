Exotic Vehicles

There are many types of exotic vehicles. What makes them different from the cars you would see on the road is that they could have a unique engine and can be a design that looks too “weird” to be true.

Exotic Vehicles are typically found to be manufactured within the country where it was designed, unlike Toyota, where we create vehicles here (Canada) and get them manufactured (most of the time) in Japan. There are many makes and models, though we will go through some of the more “popular” types of exotic cars.

Koenigsegg

Koenigsegg is an art of luxury. These vehicles are rare to see. A few Koenigsegg vehicles include the “One:1” model, Agera, Regera, Jesko etc. These models stated they are trendy amongst the car community, mainly for their beauty and performance. Koenigsegg started in August 94’. This man was founded by Christian von Koenigsegg, passionate about creating the “ultimate” vehicle. Eventually, through the work of his first car, the “CC,” Koenigsegg became more known throughout the world, leading to making cars like the Agera, which has broken the record of 400km/h.

Lamborghini

Lamborghini is a very well-known manufacturer. Known for its speed and aggressiveness, Lamborghini has exceeded the expectations of wealth. Founded in May 63’, in Italy, Lamborghini started as a small company. Later it was bought by Volkswagen 98’. Soon the company is known worldwide, known throughout the world as one of the wealthiest, most luxurious car manufacturers ever. Well-known vehicles of Lamborghini include the Aventador, Huracan, and the Urus.

Bugatti

The brand started like any other, founded in France in 1909 by Ettore Bugatti. Bugatti is now known as a luxury/sports car manufacturer, creating vehicles that exceed 300km/h. Bugatti’s well-known models are the Chiron, Veyron, Divo, La Voiture Noire, etc. Bugatti is also owned by Volkswagen, which makes it a sister company to Lamborghini.

Ferrari

The Ferrari La Ferrari is a class car that everyone should know. Ferrari was founded in 1939 by Enzo Ferrari. The company’s first car was produced in 1947. The company was soon partially owned by the FCA (Fiat Chrysler Automobiles); soon after, this company separated from the FCA, eventually becoming known as who they are today. Popular Ferrari models created are the Ferrari La Ferrari, Ferrari 488 Pista, Ferrari 812, Ferrari 458 Italia, etc. Ferrari also competes in F1 racing, designing vehicles to compete in high-speed racing.

Pagani

Pagani is a reticent company. It has yet to be heard of, though the people who know it also know the company makes beautiful cars. Focusing more on luxury and looks rather than performance is a winner in this case. Pagani was founded in 92’ by Horacio Pagani. Pagani is a man with creativity. The vehicles he has created are far from the base of where Bugatti stands. These vehicles include the Zonda R, Huayra R, Zonda Roadster F, etc.

Porsche

Porche is a company that everyone knows. Founded in Germany, 48’ Porche began after getting inspiration from Volkswagen. Unlike Volkswagen, Porsche created cars that looked nice, and as the years went by, Porsche is now considered equal to Rolls-Royce. It’s luxury and performance combined, making it even.
